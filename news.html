News & Updates

Research findings, industry developments, and ILDA announcements.

June 2025
ILDA Releases First Global Memorial Diamond Consumer Protection Report

A comprehensive analysis of 47 consumer complaints across 12 jurisdictions, identifying common red flags and establishing baseline expectations for ethical memorial diamond services. The report recommends mandatory cooling-off periods and standardized disclosure forms for all sales. Key findings: 34% of complaints involved laboratories that issued their own certificates without third-party verification.

May 2025
Silmaril Diamonds Achieves Full ILDA Laboratory Accreditation

After 18 months of rigorous auditing, Silmaril becomes the first Southern Hemisphere laboratory to receive full ILDA accreditation for origin validation and spectroscopic grading. The facility in Auckland passed unannounced inspections with zero non-compliance findings. Silmaril's proprietary carbon extraction method achieved 99.7% purity in ILDA testing.

April 2025
BioGem Lab Passes Full ILDA Audit — HPHT Synthesis Certified

BioGem Lab, based in Luoyang, China, has completed full accreditation after demonstrating compliance across all four pillars. The laboratory's HPHT presses operated at 5.5 GPa and 1,400°C with deviation tolerances well within ILDA thresholds. BioGem Lab becomes the second facility globally to receive full ILDA certification.

March 2025
New HPHT Calibration Protocol V.1 Adopted by All Member Labs

Updated temperature and pressure tolerance standards go into effect, requiring quarterly third-party audits of press calibration across all accredited facilities. The new protocol was developed in collaboration with ETH Zurich's High-Pressure Physics Laboratory. Key change: maximum allowable deviation reduced from 3% to 2% for runs exceeding 30 days.

February 2025
Ethics Review Panel Issues First Public Adjudication

The independent Ethics Review Panel has published its first public decision, ruling in favor of a consumer who disputed the spectroscopic grading of a memorial diamond. The Panel ordered a full re-analysis at an independent facility, which confirmed the consumer's concerns. The laboratory in question was issued a formal warning and given 90 days to implement corrective measures.

January 2025
ILDA Global Registry Enters Beta Testing

The public certificate verification system is now in beta with 12 test dossiers from accredited laboratories. Registry v1.0 will support dossier lookup, spectroscopic data download, and certification status verification. Full public launch scheduled for Q3 2025.
