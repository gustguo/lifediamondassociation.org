Mission & Vision

Building a transparent, trustworthy, and sustainable life diamond industry.

Our Mission

The International Life Diamond Association (ILDA) is an independent, non-profit association dedicated to establishing and maintaining rigorous transparency standards for the life diamond industry. We certify that diamonds marketed as being created from biological sources truly originate from the claimed carbon material through an auditable, transparent chain-of-custody process.

We are not a laboratory. We do not manufacture or sell diamonds. We are an independent third-party organization that audits, verifies, and certifies the traceability claims of life diamond producers, ensuring that consumers can trust what they are told about the origin of their diamonds.

Core Principles

  • Independence
    No commercial ties to diamond production or sales
  • Transparency
    Public verification of all certificates
  • Rigorous Standards
    Annual audits and documented chain-of-custody
  • Consumer Protection
    Empowering buyers with verified information

Our Vision

A world where every life diamond's origin is independently verifiable and consumers can trust the stories behind their stones.

Independence Statement

ILDA is legally and operationally independent from any life diamond manufacturer or retailer. Our funding comes from membership dues and certification fees, not from diamond sales commissions. This structural independence ensures that our certification decisions are based solely on documentation quality and process compliance, never on commercial relationships.

Our board members are required to disclose any financial interests in life diamond businesses, and recuse themselves from decisions affecting those entities. Annual financial audits ensure our continued independence and non-profit status.
