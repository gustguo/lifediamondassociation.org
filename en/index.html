Our Mission

Independent, transparent, and rigorous — the three pillars of ILDA's commitment to the life diamond industry.

Independent Certification

As a non-profit association with no commercial stake in diamond sales, ILDA provides truly independent third-party verification of traceability claims.

Transparent Standards

We establish and publish clear industry standards for traceability documentation, laboratory audit protocols, and consumer disclosure requirements.

Public Verification

Every certified diamond receives a unique traceability certificate. Anyone can verify its authenticity through our public online portal at any time.
