News & Updates

Research findings, industry developments, and ILDA announcements.

June 2025
ILDA Releases First Global Memorial Diamond Consumer Protection Report

A comprehensive analysis of 47 consumer complaints across 12 jurisdictions, identifying common red flags and establishing baseline expectations for ethical memorial diamond services. The report recommends mandatory cooling-off periods and standardized disclosure forms for all sales.

May 2025
Silmaril Diamonds Achieves Full ILDA Laboratory Accreditation

After 18 months of rigorous auditing, Silmaril becomes the first Southern Hemisphere laboratory to receive full ILDA accreditation for origin validation and spectroscopic grading. The facility in Auckland passed unannounced inspections with zero non-compliance findings.

April 2025
New Raman Spectroscopy Protocol Published in Journal of Gemmology

The ILDA Technical Advisory Committee's revised Raman analysis protocol has been peer-reviewed and published, establishing new benchmarks for distinguishing memorial diamonds from synthetic diamonds of industrial origin. Member laboratories have 6 months to implement the new standards.

March 2025
New HPHT Calibration Protocol V.1 Adopted by All Member Labs

Updated temperature and pressure tolerance standards go into effect, requiring quarterly third-party audits of press calibration across all accredited facilities. The new protocol was developed in collaboration with ETH Zurich's High-Pressure Physics Laboratory.

February 2025
Ethics Review Panel Issues First Public Adjudication

The independent Ethics Review Panel has published its first public decision, ruling in favor of a consumer who disputed the spectroscopic grading of a memorial diamond. The Panel ordered a full re-analysis at an independent facility, which confirmed the consumer's concerns.

January 2025
BioGem Lab Joins ILDA as Provisional Member

BioGem Lab, based in Luoyang, China, has been granted provisional accreditation after successfully passing ILDA audits for Origin Validation and Elemental Purity. The laboratory specializes in HPHT diamond synthesis from carbonized organic materials.
