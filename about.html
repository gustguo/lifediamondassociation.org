About ILDA

An independent, non-profit association founded to bring transparency, accountability, and consumer trust to the life diamond industry.

Our Mission

The life diamond industry — transforming biological carbon from hair, fur, or ashes into genuine laboratory-grown diamonds — has grown rapidly. Yet consumers have had no independent way to verify that their diamond truly came from the claimed source.

ILDA was founded to fill this gap. We are not a laboratory. We do not sell diamonds. We are an independent third-party association that audits, certifies, and verifies the traceability claims of life diamond manufacturers.

"Every life diamond deserves a transparent origin story that consumers can independently verify."

What Makes ILDA Different

  • Independent: No financial stake in diamond sales
  • Transparent: Public verification of every certificate
  • Rigorous: Annual laboratory audits and document verification
  • Global: Open to laboratories worldwide that meet our standards

Governance

ILDA is a Hong Kong-registered non-profit association governed by an independent board.

Hong Kong Registration

Registered association under Hong Kong law, operating as an independent non-governmental organization with no commercial ties to diamond sales.

Independent Board

Governed by a board of directors with backgrounds in gemology, materials science, consumer advocacy, and ethics — not diamond retail.

Annual Audits

Member laboratories undergo annual third-party audits of their chain-of-custody documentation and process records.
