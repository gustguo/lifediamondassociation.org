What ILDA Does

We are an independent non-profit association dedicated to bringing transparency and trust to the life diamond industry through rigorous third-party certification.

Third-Party Certification

Independent verification of carbon source, laboratory process, and gemological specifications. No vested interest in sales — only in truth.

Public Verification

Every certified diamond receives a unique traceability certificate with a QR code and online verification portal. Anyone can confirm authenticity instantly.

Standard Setting

We establish and maintain industry standards for traceability documentation, laboratory audit protocols, and consumer disclosure requirements.

How Traceability Works

A rigorous, transparent process from carbon source to certified diamond.

1

Carbon Receipt

Biological sample logged with unique chain-of-custody ID

2

Lab Processing

Carbon extraction and diamond synthesis under documented conditions

3

Gemological Grading

Independent gemological analysis (IGI/GIA) with documented parameters

4

ILDA Certification

Traceability certificate issued with unique verification code
