The International Life Diamond Association (ILDA) stands as the supreme independent authority in the scientific authentication and ethical certification of memorial diamonds. We do not manufacture or sell. Our sole purpose is to protect consumers and establish unyielding frameworks that guarantee the exact origin, elemental purity, and ethical synthesis of diamonds cultivated from carbon signatures.

ILDA operates as an independent non-profit association. We protect the integrity of your most profound memories through rigorous third-party certification that no laboratory can influence.

The ILDA Core Standard

Every laboratory and agency operating under ILDA accreditation must adhere strictly to a quadruple-verified scientific protocol. Compromise is not an option.

Protocol V.1 — 2025
I.

Origin Validation

Cryptographic tracking of organic materials from the point of handover, ensuring an indisputable chain of custody from source to certification.

II.

Elemental Purity

Mandatory extraction thresholds demanding verified carbon purity before the crystallization phase can commence, measured by independent labs.

III.

HPHT Synthesis

Stringent calibration audits of High-Pressure High-Temperature presses to mirror exact natural lithospheric conditions within certified tolerances.

IV.

Spectroscopy

Final non-destructive Raman and infrared spectroscopic analysis to verify atomic lattice structure and issue the ILDA grading dossier.
