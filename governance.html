Governance

A transparent structure designed to prevent conflicts of interest and maintain the highest standards of integrity.

Organizational Structure

Three-Pillar Governance

ILDA operates under a deliberately fragmented governance structure. No single individual or entity can control both the certification process and the standards-setting process.

Board of Directors

The Board of Directors oversees the strategic direction and financial sustainability of ILDA. Board members are elected by the General Assembly for three-year terms, with a maximum of two consecutive terms.

Critically, no Board member may hold a direct financial interest in any memorial diamond laboratory, and all members must disclose any material relationships with industry participants annually.

Technical Advisory Committee (TAC)

The TAC is responsible for developing, reviewing, and updating all scientific protocols. It is composed of independent scientists with expertise in:

  • Spectroscopy (Raman, FTIR, UV-Vis)
  • High-pressure materials science
  • Carbon chemistry and isotope analysis
  • Crystallography and gemology
  • Forensic chain-of-custody protocols

TAC members are appointed by the Board on the recommendation of an independent scientific nomination panel. Their tenure is not contingent on any commercial relationship.

Current TAC Research Priorities

2025-2026 Work Plan:

1. Develop standardized protocol for carbon isotope ratio analysis (¹³C/¹²C) to distinguish biogenic from industrial carbon sources in memorial diamonds.

2. Establish baseline spectroscopic signatures for diamonds synthesized from different biological materials (human hair, animal fur, cremation ashes).

3. Create a reference library of synthetic diamond markers using photoluminescence mapping at cryogenic temperatures.

Ethics Review Panel

The Ethics Review Panel investigates complaints against ILDA-accredited laboratories and arbitrates disputes between consumers and certified members. It operates independently of both the Board and the TAC, with its own budget line approved by the General Assembly.

Financial Transparency

ILDA publishes annual audited financial statements within 90 days of fiscal year-end. Our primary revenue sources are membership dues from accredited laboratories and application fees from new certification candidates. We do not accept donations from commercial entities engaged in memorial diamond manufacturing.
