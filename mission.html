Our Mission

Establishing trust in the life diamond industry through independent certification, scientific rigor, and unwavering ethical standards.

What We Stand For

Independent. Transparent. Rigorous.

The International Life Diamond Association was founded on a single conviction: consumers deserve to know, without doubt, that the memorial diamond they hold contains the carbon signature of their loved one.

We do not manufacture. We do not sell. We exist solely to certify, verify, and protect.

Core Principles

Independence

ILDA is a non-profit association with no financial ties to any diamond manufacturing laboratory. Our certification decisions cannot be bought, influenced, or expedited.

Transparency

Every certified diamond's full dossier — spectroscopic data, origin chain, and grading report — is accessible through the Global Registry for independent verification.

Scientific Rigor

Our standards are built on peer-reviewed spectroscopic methods, not marketing claims. Every protocol is open to scrutiny and revision as technology advances.

What We Do Not Do

In an industry rife with conflicts of interest, our boundaries are clear:

  • We do not manufacture memorial diamonds
  • We do not sell diamonds or carbon collection kits
  • We do not accept referral fees from laboratories
  • We do not certify our own members' products without independent third-party verification
  • We do not engage in marketing or promotion on behalf of any commercial entity

Why Hong Kong

ILDA is registered in Hong Kong SAR, China, as a non-profit association. This jurisdiction was chosen for its robust legal framework governing international standards bodies, its position as a neutral ground between East and West, and its commitment to the rule of law.

Our presence in Hong Kong reflects the global nature of the life diamond industry — laboratories in Europe, North America, Asia, and Oceanica all operate under the same ILDA standards, ensuring consistency regardless of geography.
