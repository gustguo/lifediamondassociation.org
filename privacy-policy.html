Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

The International Life Diamond Association (ILDA) operates the Global Registry and this website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Registry Users: When you verify a certificate, we do not collect personal information. The dossier number alone is sufficient to retrieve public certification data.

Contact Form Submissions: If you email us through the contact form, we retain your name, email address, and message content for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Laboratory Accreditation Applicants: We collect laboratory registration details, staff credentials, and facility documentation solely for the accreditation review process.

3. What We Do Not Collect

ILDA does not and will not:

  • Collect names, addresses, or contact information of memorial diamond consumers or the deceased
  • Store financial transaction data from any purchase
  • Track your browsing behavior across other websites
  • Use cookies for advertising or profiling purposes

4. Global Registry Data

The Global Registry contains only the following public information:

  • Certification status and dossier number
  • Accredited laboratory name and ILDA ID
  • Spectroscopic data (Raman, FTIR, UV-Vis results)
  • General origin material type (e.g., hair, ashes) without personal identifiers

Personal names, geographic addresses, purchaser information, and specific facility locations beyond country level are never stored in or accessible through the Registry.

5. Data Retention

Certification data in the Global Registry is retained indefinitely to preserve the integrity of the verification system. Contact form submissions are retained for 24 months and then deleted. Accreditation application materials are retained for the duration of the laboratory's membership plus 5 years for compliance purposes.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

ILDA does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. We disclose information only when:

  • Required by applicable law or valid court order
  • Necessary to investigate a complaint filed with the Ethics Review Panel
  • The information is already public through the Global Registry

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, and request deletion of information no longer required for our operational purposes. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@lifediamondassociation.org.

8. Security

ILDA implements industry-standard security measures to protect the Global Registry and all stored data. Registry access is via HTTPS encryption. Spectroscopic data is stored on encrypted servers with access limited to authorized ILDA technical staff.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Significant changes will be announced via the News section.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact info@lifediamondassociation.org.
