Understanding Accreditation Status

Accredited: The laboratory has completed all four pillars of the ILDA audit and maintains quarterly compliance reporting. Certificates issued carry full ILDA-backed guarantees.

Provisional: The laboratory has passed initial audits but is within its first 12-month evaluation period. Full accreditation is granted after sustained compliance.

Independent: This laboratory is known to operate in the memorial diamond industry but has not applied for or received ILDA accreditation. ILDA does not endorse their claims.
