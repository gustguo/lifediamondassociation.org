Understanding Accreditation Status

Full Accreditation: The laboratory has completed all four pillars of the ILDA audit and maintains quarterly compliance reporting. Certificates issued carry full ILDA-backed guarantees.

Provisional Accreditation: The laboratory has passed initial audits but is within its first 12-month evaluation period. Full accreditation is granted after sustained compliance.

Under Review:>/strong> The laboratory has submitted an application and is undergoing the ILDA audit process. No certificates may be issued until accreditation is granted.
