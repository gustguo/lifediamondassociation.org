Membership

Join ILDA as a certified laboratory and demonstrate your commitment to traceability and transparency.

Membership Types

ILDA offers three tiers of membership, each with specific rights and obligations.

Associate Member

For laboratories in the process of achieving full certification. Provides access to standards documentation and educational resources.

  • Access to ILDA standards
  • Educational materials
  • Newsletter subscription
  • Cannot issue ILDA certificates

Certified Laboratory

Full certification with the authority to issue ILDA traceability certificates. Requires annual audit and compliance.

  • Issue ILDA certificates
  • Listed in public directory
  • Use ILDA certification mark
  • Annual audit required

Board Member

Leadership position for senior representatives of certified laboratories. Board members guide association strategy.

  • Strategic decision-making
  • Standards committee participation
  • Vote in General Assembly
  • 3-year term, elected

Why Join ILDA?

Benefits of becoming a certified member laboratory.

Consumer Trust

ILDA certification is a mark of independent verification. Consumers can verify your certificates through our public portal, building confidence in your traceability claims.

Industry Recognition

Stand out in a crowded market. ILDA-certified laboratories are listed in our public directory, accessible to consumers and retailers seeking trustworthy partners.

Standards Participation

Shape the future of the industry. Certified members participate in the Technical Committee, contributing to the evolution of the ILDA Traceability Standard.

Educational Resources

Access exclusive training materials, audit preparation guides, and best practice documentation developed by industry experts.

Ready to Apply?

The certification process typically takes 60-90 days, including documentation review, on-site audit, and board approval.
