Apply for Membership

Begin the certification process to become an ILDA-certified laboratory.

Application Process

Four stages from initial application to full certification.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documentation.

2

Documentation Review

ILDA reviews your chain-of-custody documentation and quality systems.

3

On-Site Audit

An ILDA auditor visits your facility to verify processes and documentation.

4

Board Approval

The Technical Committee reviews findings and the Board grants certification.

Requirements

To qualify for ILDA certification, laboratories must:

  • Operate a life diamond manufacturing facility
  • Maintain documented chain-of-custody records
  • Have carbon source identification and preservation protocols
  • Implement quality control and traceability systems
  • Agree to annual audits and compliance reviews
  • Accept ILDA's independence and ethics requirements
